Our Services

At Muskoka Home Framing, we are continually refining our skills and updating our knowledge of cost efficient materials and technology related to methods of building.

Starting a new home from the foundation up, we maximize building material to achieve sustainability and minimize waste of material which ultimately saves the client money. Our restoration capabilities have also become a strong aspect of our company, which has been achieved through the increasing experience gained from the framing of homes.

Our priority is getting the job done within the expected time as per contract and to the complete satisfaction of the client.

The job is executed with utmost integrity, safety and professionalism. Word of mouth referrals continue to contribute to our ever growing number of satisfied clients.
Muskoka Home Framing is ongoing in identifying and fulfilling the applicable by-laws and OBC-building codes. We are fully insured and WSIB compliant.

About Muskoka New Home Framing

Eric and his crew have more than 20 years experience in framing construction of homes, which also includes restorations and additions.
